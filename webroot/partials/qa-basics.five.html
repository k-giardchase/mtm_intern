Behavior Driven Development + Behat

This next section covers briefly our testing philosophy at Metal Toad and how that translates into acceptance criteria and automated tests. Behavior Driven Development is based mainly on a mix of Test Driven Development and Domain-Driven Design. Ultimately this means a shared domain specific language understood by both the development team and business owners while using the BDD framework. You could spend a long time unpacking those three different methodologies but I want to focus on how this pertains to testing.
