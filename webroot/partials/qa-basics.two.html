Configure JIRA Capture

Once you are able to login to the Metal Toad JIRA website and able to create issues lets configure JIRA Capture. Follow the steps located below:

  • Click the JIRA Capture icon in the upper right hand corner of your browser (blue person with three dots above it) to open the extension.
  • Next click the settings icon (grey gear) if the configuration fields are not already visible on the left hand side of the your browser.
  • Fill in Server URL with metaltoad.atlassian.net
  • Fill in Username with your supplied login username for JIRA
  • Fill in Password with your supplied login password for JIRA
  • Click the save button
